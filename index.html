DOTOCOME 以"Power the AI Revolution"为使命，对标台达、光宝等全球头部电源企业技术标准，为中国及全球客户提供具备国际竞争力的AI服务器电源产品与系统级供电方案。

DOTOCOME is a high-tech enterprise specializing in AI infrastructure power solutions, dedicated to providing high power density, high efficiency, and high reliability CRPS server power supplies for next-generation AI data centers. Our product line covers the full power range from 800W to 5500W, supports both air cooling and liquid cooling, and actively develops 800V HVDC high-voltage DC power distribution architecture.

With the mission "Power the AI Revolution", DOTOCOME benchmarks against global leaders like Delta and Lite-On, delivering internationally competitive AI server power products and system-level solutions to customers in China and worldwide.

06 应用场景与解决方案Application Scenarios

🖥️ 场景一：GPU AI训练集群Scenario 1: GPU AI Training Cluster

需求：单节点8×H200/B200 GPU，总功耗 ~10kW，需54V高压直流供电，支持热插拔冗余
方案：DOTOCOME CRPS-3200 钛金 × 4 (3+1冗余) 或 CRPS-5500 超钛金 × 2 (1+1冗余)
优势：96.5%+ 转换效率，PMBus全数字监控，支持GPU峰值负载动态响应

Requirement: Single node 8×H200/B200 GPU, ~10kW total, 54V high-voltage DC, hot-swap redundancy
Solution: DOTOCOME CRPS-3200 Titanium × 4 (3+1 redundant) or CRPS-5500 Ultra-Titanium × 2 (1+1 redundant)
Advantage: 96.5%+ efficiency, PMBus full digital monitoring, dynamic GPU peak load response

🏢 场景二：NVIDIA GB200 NVL72 机柜Scenario 2: NVIDIA GB200 NVL72 Rack

需求：单机柜 72×B200 GPU，总功耗 ~264kW，需系统级供电
方案：DOTOCOME Power Shelf 33kW × 8组 (5+1冗余每组) = 264kW总供电
优势：液冷散热，97%+系统效率，Shelf Manager集中管理，支持热插拔维护

Requirement: Single rack 72×B200 GPU, ~264kW total, system-level power delivery required
Solution: DOTOCOME Power Shelf 33kW × 8 groups (5+1 redundant each) = 264kW total
Advantage: Liquid cooling, 97%+ system efficiency, Shelf Manager centralized control, hot-swap maintenance

🌐 场景三：液冷数据中心Scenario 3: Liquid-Cooled Data Center

需求：低PUE (<1.15)，全液冷环境，电源需兼容浸没式或冷板式散热
方案：DOTOCOME 液冷系列 (1600W/3000W/5500W) + 浸没式液冷版
优势：专为液冷环境设计，无风扇或低噪音风扇，支持最高65°C进液温度

Requirement: Low PUE (<1.15), full liquid cooling environment, PSU compatible with immersion or cold plate
Solution: DOTOCOME Liquid Cooling Series (1600W/3000W/5500W) + Immersion variants
Advantage: Purpose-built for liquid cooling, fanless or low-noise fans, supports up to 65°C inlet temperature

📡 场景四：边缘AI推理节点Scenario 4: Edge AI Inference Nodes

需求：分布式边缘部署，4-8卡推理节点，需宽温、高可靠性
方案：DOTOCOME CRPS-2000/3000 宽温版 (-5°C ~ 55°C)
优势：宽输入电压(100-240V AC)，适应边缘电力波动，MTBF > 1,000,000小时

Requirement: Distributed edge deployment, 4-8 card inference nodes, wide temp, high reliability
Solution: DOTOCOME CRPS-2000/3000 Wide-Temp Edition (-5°C ~ 55°C)
Advantage: Wide input voltage (100-240V AC), tolerates edge power fluctuations, MTBF > 1,000,000 hrs
